# Japanese Honorific Appropriateness Rubric

Score from 0.0 to 1.0 in 0.05 increments.

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1.00  | Register is fully appropriate for a neutral business or written context. Polite forms (です/ます) are used consistently. No tonal mismatches. |
| 0.85  | Mostly appropriate; minor inconsistencies (e.g. mixing です/ます with plain form within the same paragraph). |
| 0.60  | Register is recognizable but inconsistent or pitched too high/low for the context. |
| 0.30  | Tonally jarring (e.g. casual forms in formal contexts, or excessive 敬語 in casual ones). |
| 0.00  | Severe register breakdown; reads as unnatural or insulting. |

The judge should respond with **only the numeric score** on a single line.
