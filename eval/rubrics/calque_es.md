# Spanish Calque-Free Rubric

Score from 0.0 to 1.0 in 0.05 increments. Higher = fewer calque artifacts.

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1.00  | Reads as natural Spanish prose. No literal-translation artifacts from English. |
| 0.85  | Slight English flavor in word choice; native speakers would understand without strain. |
| 0.60  | Occasional calques (e.g. "tomar control" instead of "controlar"), but meaning is clear. |
| 0.30  | Heavy English-shaped phrasing; awkward to read in Spanish. |
| 0.00  | Word-for-word translation; many constructions are not idiomatic Spanish at all. |

The judge should respond with **only the numeric score** on a single line.
